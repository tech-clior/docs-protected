The Art of Yukata

Effortless Japanese elegance for your summer wardrobe. Experience the modern evolution of traditional summer kimono, designed for easy wearing and easy care.

A New Summer Standard

The Yukata is a lightweight, casual summer kimono that has graced Japanese festivals and warm summer evenings for centuries. Today, it has evolved into a versatile garment that brings an air of elegance to any summer outing or relaxing day at home.

For European enthusiasts, wearing a kimono may seem intimidating. Our curated collection offers "Pre-tailored" (お仕立て済) pieces, meticulously designed so you can slip them on with ease and enjoy luxurious, highly breathable comfort without compromising on traditional style.

IKS COLLECTION & Ryobijin

Crafted by Enomoto Co., Ltd., an esteemed textile trading company with over 80 years of history in Hamamatsu, the IKS COLLECTION stands on three pillars: authentic Fabric, intuitive Usability, and high-sense Design.

This collection proudly features Ryobijin (涼美人)—a modern marvel of textile engineering. This lightweight, dry-touch polyester provides a gorgeous drape, complete freedom of movement, and is entirely wrinkle-resistant and machine washable. It's the perfect harmony of traditional beauty and modern convenience.
